Overview

  • Daily production loop: client inputs → direction rough → editor handoff → rapid iteration → internal acceptance → client review → publish.
  • Two-editor model enables parallel cuts and faster experiments on hooks, pacing, and retention.
  • Lightweight templates and versioning keep assets consistent across brands.
Premiere Pro After Effects Editor handoff

Weekly → Daily loop

  1. Weekly kickoff (client call)

    Align themes, priorities, references, and the week’s target outputs.

  2. Daily production loop

    Direction & naming → two editors in parallel passes → quick checks until publish-ready.

  3. Mid-week checkpoint

    Async review to swap hooks, adjust pacing, and re-prioritize clips to maximize retention.

  4. Publish & reset

    Queue assets for posting, archive with templates, prep inputs for next week’s kickoff.
