Zen Video Agency

Streamlined editing workflows for creators and brands.

Built Aug 2025; repeatable intake → edit → review loop with two remote editors and a weekly rhythm so quality improves every cycle.

Visit Agency Site Email

Overview

  • Daily production loop: client inputs → direction rough → editor handoff → rapid iteration → acceptance → client final pass → publish.
  • Two-editor model enables parallel cuts and faster experiments on hooks, pacing, and retention.
  • Lightweight templates and versioning keep assets consistent across brands.

Weekly → Daily loop

  • Weekly kickoff (client call)

    Align themes, priorities, references, and target outputs for the week.

  • Daily production loop

    Direction & naming → two editors in parallel passes → quick checks until a review-ready cut emerges.

  • Mid-week checkpoint

    Async review to swap hooks, adjust pacing, and re-prioritize clips to maximize retention.

  • Publish & reset

    Assets versioned to templates, queued for posting, and inputs readied for the next kickoff.

Delivery cadence

Retainer clients: 5 Short-form/day: ~4 Long-form/week: ~2
