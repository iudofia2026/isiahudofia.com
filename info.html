I'm Isiah Udofia, originally from Montclair, New Jersey and now at Yale University.


‍

I'm a product-minded engineer designing end-to-end user experiences, from problem framing to shipped interfaces. I also bring working proficiency in video, photo, and animation, which I integrate directly into my design work.


‍
